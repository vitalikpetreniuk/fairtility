Our mission

Fairtility’s mission is to provide embryologist and RE’s the tools they need to make the smartest decision that will result in better patient care. Artificial intelligence technology has been successfully applied to identify objects within an image and predict shapes In medicine mainly in pathology, radiology and ultrasound. Our focus is on building AI applications for fertility.

Our primary goal is to provide Infertility With Automated, Accurate & Invisible By The Human Eye Embryo Classificartion And Evaluation

Only by adopting new technology that significantly enhances the capabilities of IVF clinics, can the ever growing demand for IVF treatments be mitigated. Fairtility empowers Embryologists with our revolutionary KID+ offering which helps clinics chose the best embryo for implantation.
