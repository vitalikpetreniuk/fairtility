The application of advanced AI and computer vision technology to the embryo quality assessment process. Quality assessment is performed today by embryologist based on a subjective, non-scientific and inconsistent process. This process involves manual/human inspection of embryo images and a grading system which is based on a qualitative assessment of a handful of markers such as cell symmetry, level of cell fragmentation etc. The introduction of AI and big-data is nothing short of groundbreaking, as it inserts science and statistical analysis into the process, resulting in a phenomenal improvement in prediction accuracy - from the typical human accuracy of 55-60% to higher than 90% by the computer.  The application of advanced AI and computer vision technology to the embryo quality assessment process.
