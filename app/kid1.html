KID+ (for time lapse)

KID+, using advanced machine learning, captures and automatically annotates embryo developmental events with accurate time stamps. It also monitors for any abnormal development patterns and flags these irregularities. Once KID+ has “learned” more about the embryo from watching its development, it scores the embryo and ranks it among the patient’s other embryos, enabling embryologists to easily pick the best possible embryo for implantation.

“We are here to help you make the smarter descision”

We know that embryologists have a very tough job. KID+ is here to help by tracking, annotating, and scoring embryo development, flagging abnormalities, ranking the embryos, and recommending the most optimal transfer plan to increase pregnancy success.

Automatic Annotation
precise evaluation of the timing of cellular divisions and the detection of normal and abnormal hallmarks of embryo evelopment Morphokinetic parameters of the embryo are known to be correlated with implantation potential and can be used as predictors of embryo quality. Leveraging video data captured by time lapse incubators, these predictive models, which are based on a combination of statistical analysis and AI methods, can be used to predict embryo implantation. AI is able to identify nuances, better the human eye and learns the subtle parameters associated with each developmental event. KID+ “watches” the embryos as they develop and tracks the timing or kinetics in between each developmental event. Because KID+ has been trained on an extensive dataset and has learned how morphokinetic features found in each frame are associated with what outcomes, it uses timing between each event to calculate the likelihood of blast formation and implantation.
